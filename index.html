Would the cats astronauts?

Cats are fascinating animals surrounded by mysteries. With an independent and mysterious temperament, these felines are known for their agility, elegance, and often unpredictable behavior. Recently, however, a theory has been gaining ground on the internet and among conspiracy theorists: Are cats astronauts?

The theory suggests that cats are not land animals, but rather extraterrestrial beings sent to carry out a specific mission on our planet. There are historical accounts that point to the fact that cats were considered sacred beings in different cultures and even associated with divine figures. This could indicate that cats have a much more mystical origin than previously thought.

In addition, there are theorists who believe that felines possess telepathic abilities and are able to communicate with other alien species. This belief is based on reports from people who claim to have seen cats reacting to stimuli that are not perceptible to humans, or even behaving in unusual ways during astronomical events such as an aurora borealis. These and other clues were interpreted as signs that cats were interplanetary beings.
